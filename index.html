About us

You may imagine the average gamer sitting in a dark room, grinding and tryharding, all to just end up second at best anyways. Sadly, this is the fate awaiting many lonely gamers. This is why BigChungus esports has been founded; to bring light into the darkness of the competitive esports environment (no we are not talking about Leonas ult).

We think that the L in Gaming stands for lonely and that's why we aim to bring the gaming community together. Skill is measured in fun and losses are celebrated as enthusiastically as wins. So, open a beer (or another beverage of your choice), grab a glizzy (or another snack of your choice) and join us :)
