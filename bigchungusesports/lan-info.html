BigChungus and Friends LAN

General Information

BYOC: You have to bring your own computer to our LAN. Sadly, there won't be no possibility to rent one.

Travel:
It is possible to buy a parking ticket in our ticket store and we will have enough parking slots right next to the event location. Furthermore, the event location is right next to a train station (Biberist Ost), where you have direct train connections to Bern, Burgdorf, Thun and Solothurn.

Food and Drinks:
We will have a Bar, where we offer drinks (alcoholic as well as non-alcoholic) and snacks. In particular, thanks to our sponsor, we will be able to offer El Tony Mate to extra discount prices! There is also a Coop restaurant about 200 m distance from the LAN location and there are various takeaway options in the region.

Resting and Showering: We will provide space for you to rest. Please note that this space is limited; therefore please only bring a 90cm mattress for yourself rather than a large queensize one. There will be indoor showers, and because we expect high outside temperatures, we will also provide outdoor showers.

Air conditioning: We acknowledge the fact that a room filled with PCs in August can get quite hot, that's why we will have an air conditioning system to cool the LAN down a bit.

Communication:
Join our Discord server (LINK) to stay up to date and ask questions (and find new friends :) )
