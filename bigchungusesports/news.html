Big Chungus internal LAN 2024

03/01/2024

BigChungus esports will run its first internal LAN between the 12th and 14th of January. The LAN will serve as a testbed for future LAN events which BigChungus esports plans on hosting in the upcoming years.

Big Chungus at SwitzerLAN 2023

10/10/2023

BigChungus esports will be attending SwitzerLAN 2023 between the 12th and 16th of October. Everybody is looking forward to it, especially since this year will feature the collaboration with GRING elektroSport.
