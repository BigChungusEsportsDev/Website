BigChungus and Friends LAN

Tourneys

Main Tourneys: Because our LAN has only 100 members, we try to adapt our tournaments to the wishes of our participants. We have a channel on our discord where you can emoji react to different games and if a game has enough thumbs-ups, then we will organise a tournament!

(Big) Chungionship: Prove that you are the biggest Chungus! The Chungionship is a tourney stretching over the entire LAN where you can prove your worth in teams of 2-3 players by playing small fun-tournaments and completing epic challenges. You will have to display your gaming skills (of course), but also your sports skills and your creativity. We have something for everyone :D
You can find more information here.

Small Tourneys: There will be several small fun-tourneys included in the Chungionship. If you are participant of the Chungionship, you will be granted points for participating and winning. However, you can also participate just for fun, if you are not part of any Chungionship team. The small tourneys will not be part of the award ceremony and the winners will be listed on Discord.

Prizes: The winners of all main tourneys and of the Chungionship will recieve a Big Chungus Mystery Chest containing surprise gifts.
