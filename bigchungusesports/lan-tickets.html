Visitor Tickets

Curious about our LAN Party but can't commit to the full five days? Not sure if a LAN party is right for you and want to get a taste of the experience?

We have the perfect solution for you! Get one of our visitor tickets and enjoy a full day of access to the BigChungus and Friends LAN. This is a great opportunity to visit your friends, meet new people, and immerse yourself in the exciting world of LAN gaming. You'll be able to join in the fun, whether it's playing a game of Flunky Ball, watching our participants compete in tournaments, or just soaking up the vibrant atmosphere.

A visitor ticket is only CHF 20 and includes admission to the LAN Hall for one day. And if you get hungry, we've got you covered with a free meal included.

Don't miss your chance to see what our LAN party is all about. We can't wait to see you!
