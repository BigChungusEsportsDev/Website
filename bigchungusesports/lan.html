General Information

Time and Date

The LAN will start Wednesday 31.07 17:00 and ends 04.08 15:00

Location and Travel

The LAN is located at: Emmenstrasse 3, 4562 Biberist

It is possible to buy a parking ticket in our ticket store and we will have enough parking slots right next to the event location. Furthermore, the event location is right next to a train station (Biberist Ost), where you have direct train connections to Bern, Burgdorf, Thun and Solothurn.

BYOC

You have to bring your own computer to our LAN. Sadly, there won't be any possibility to rent one.

Food and Drinks

We will offer a different meal every day. There will be a vegan version too.

At our bar, we will offer alcoholic and non-alcoholic dinks as well as snacks. In particular, thanks to our sponsor, we will be able to offer El Tony Mate to extra discount prices!

Resting and Showering

We will provide space for you to rest. Please note that this space is limited; therefore please only bring a 90cm mattress for yourself rather than a large queensize one. There will be indoor showers, and because we expect high outside temperatures, we will also provide outdoor showers.

Air conditioning

We acknowledge the fact that a room filled with PCs in August can get quite hot, that's why we will have an air conditioning system to cool the LAN down a bit.

Communication

Join our Discord server to stay up to date and ask questions (and find new friends :) )
