BigChungus LAN 31.7.2025 - 03.8.2025

Tickets Available Now!

Get your ticket here!

Mark your calendars for the gaming event of the summer! From Thursday, July 31, 17:00 to Sunday, August 3, 15:00, BigChungus LAN 2025 is bringing the heat (but hopefully not too much—we’ll figure that part out). Whether you’re in to compete or just here for the vibes, this LAN is going to be something special.

Location & Travel

Biberena
Emmenstrasse 3
4562 Biberist

It is possible to buy a parking ticket in our ticket store and we will have enough parking slots right next to the event location. Furthermore, the event location is right next to a train station (Biberist Ost), where you have direct train connections to Bern, Burgdorf, Thun and Solothurn.

Bring Your Own Computer (BYOC)

As always, you’ll need to BYOC (Bring Your Own Computer), so don’t leave your rig behind! There won’t be any rentals, but rest assured we’re crafting the perfect setup for you to plug in and play.

Food & Drinks

Not sure yet what’ll be on the menu, but we’re committed to serving up delicious meals every day. Of course, we’ll include vegan options, and you can expect some cool beverages (maybe a surprise or two). Trust us, you won’t go hungry or thirsty!

Rest & Recharge

We know you’ll need breaks, so there will be dedicated resting areas. Details on sleeping arrangements will be confirmed closer to the date, but there will be room for everyone to catch some Z’s—just bring a compact mattress to save space. Showers? Yes, we’ll have those too.

Keeping It Cool

August heat? PCs everywhere? No sweat! We’re on it. Our team is figuring out the best way to keep things cool, whether it’s air conditioning, fans, or something else. We’re gamers too, so we know the struggle of overheating rigs!

Communication

Join our Discord to stay in the loop on all the latest updates, ask questions, and connect with other players. This is where you’ll hear first when we lock in the big details.
